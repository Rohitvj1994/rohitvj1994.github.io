Editorial notes

What we're looking for

Specific, honest writing about India's energy transition. Data helps. Personal experience helps. Acronyms are fine if you unpack them.

Good pieces tend to

  • Argue one clear thing
  • Cite what they claim
  • Say what they don't know
  • Land in 600–1800 words

Our process

1. You submit the form.
2. We read within a week.
3. If it fits, we email you edits.
4. Publish with your byline.

Figures & images

Mention them inline in your text (e.g., [FIGURE 1]) — we'll email you to collect the files at high resolution once we accept the piece.
